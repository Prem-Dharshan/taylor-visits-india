Taylor Swift Might Be Performing in India: Everything We Know So Far

By TechRadar Staff | Published: November 13, 2024

Is it finally happening? Taylor Swift fans in India are buzzing with excitement as rumors swirl about the possibility of the pop icon performing in the country next year. Though there has been no official announcement, insiders suggest that India may be on the list of countries for the Taylor Swift 2025 World Tour.

This wouldn’t be the first time Indian fans have clamored for a Swift concert. Over the years, her fanbase has grown exponentially in the country, with dedicated Swifties eagerly awaiting her arrival. A concert in India would mark a milestone for the music industry and could set a new precedent for international artists considering large-scale performances here.

Where Could It Happen?

According to sources, discussions have centered around major venues in Mumbai and Delhi, which have both hosted world-famous artists in recent years. The Jio World Garden in Mumbai and the Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium in Delhi are currently top contenders. Both locations are well-equipped to handle the large crowds expected to flock to see one of pop music’s biggest stars.

What to Expect from the Show

Fans who have attended Swift's past concerts know she doesn’t hold back on production value. If India makes the list, expect a show packed with stunning visuals, elaborate stage setups, and all the hits from her celebrated discography, including songs from her latest albums Midnights and Evermore.

Will India make it onto the tour map? While we're all still waiting on an official announcement, fans are already preparing for what could be one of the biggest music events in recent Indian history.
